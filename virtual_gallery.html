Candi Batu Pahat

The only major surviving monument in the Kedah settlement to be constructed of stone. According to the research,original building was the prasada type as distinct from the less massive structures often known as meru in which wood plays a much greater role. The architecture of its main structure comprises of the Hindu Siva with Vimana and Mandapa with overall measurement of 27m x 12m x 2m. Archaeological research proves that the roof is made of palm leaves with 67 pillar bases, where 39 are part of Vimana and the rest lines the Mandapa.

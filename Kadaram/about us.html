About Us

Myth Ranger

The word “Myth” are folklore genre consisting of narratives that play a fundamental role in all society. We explore the Myth throughout our history like ranger, that’s how this name come up.

Our Story

Our goal is to let people discover the beauty from our history, and we make the stories more easier for people to understand.

We explore the history and story behind the Bujang valley in Kedah.We will be introducing the Candi Lembah Bujang in an interactive way where the user can explore the site in 3 dimension and learn about its story.
